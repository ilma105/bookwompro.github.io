About BookWorm

Objective

BOOKWORM is specially for booklovers.It is an android app.It gives users the opportunity to create their own comfortable reading environment.We hope all booklovers will love this app.

More About BookWorm

By using BookWorm user can

  • select their favourite book types
  • read books
  • download free pdf
  • contribute books to the app
  • sell hard copy or soft copy of their books to other users
  • communicate with other users
  • write their thoughts in notes
  • maintain a wishlist of books
  • save their recently read book

Introducing

Features

Registration Page

  • To register in our app , You have to give a unique mail along with your full Name,UserName,Nationality,Age,password.If you already have an account , You can easily switch to Sign In page through SignIn button

SIGN IN Page

  • Sign In Details

    You can sign in to BOOKWORM by using your given mail and password. If you don't have an account , you can easily switch to Register Page through Register button

  • Forget Password?

    If you forget your passsword , no WORRY . You can easily retrive your password using your given mail . A reset password link will send to your mail . You can easily reset your password through that link




HomePage

  • Recommended For You

    Here You will find books and their pdf that have rating above four.

  • Book Genres

    There are 10 types of Book Genre . You can select your favourite genres . You can also reselect them . After selecting yours, You will find a list of books of that genre . This list will contain the name,author name, summary, rating ,cover photo , and pdf of the books. You can download your favourite books by clicking on the cover photos

  • Menu Button

    In the top right of the home page , you will find a menu button . By using the menu

    • you can go to your profile page
    • you can search other app users
    • you can note down your thoughts
    • you can find the app developers info
    • you can sign out from the app

Profile Page

  • My Profile

    In your profile you can see your profile picture , username , mail , nationality , bio . You can edit these information. You can also see your contributed book number , followers and following list

  • Wishlist

    Here you can note down the name of books which you want to read in future

  • Contribution

    Here you can contribute books to the app . To contribute a book you have to write down the book name , author name , type , rating(your own rating) and upload book pdf and then you have to click the save button to save these informations. You have to click the load button to check your contributed book list

  • Book For Sell

    Here you can sell your book to other users by giving book name , author name , type , rating(your rating) , hard copy/ soft copy , price of the book .Then you have to click the save button to save these informations. You have to click the load button to check your book sell list Other user can communicate with you for purchasing your book through chat box . You can inform them about your payment method and you can also know how to deliver them their purchased book

  • Download List

    Here you can see your downloaded book list which you have downloaded from this app .

  • Recent

    Here you can save your recently read book by giving book name,author name and book pdf . To see your recently read book you have to click load button . Then you can read that book from the app . You have an advantage here . When you read a book from recent list , the page you came out of when you go back , you will get it again . In this case you can only upload one book . If you want to upload new book , you have to delete the previous one .

  • Menu Button

    In the bottom right of the profile page , you will find a menu button . By using the menu

    • you can go to your home page
    • you can search other app users
    • you can note down your thoughts
    • you can find the app developers info
    • you can find a notification page where you will notify whenever any user send you a message or follow you

Search User

  • Here you can search any user by giving their user name in the search bar, You can also visit their profile and check their contribution list , book sell list , contributed book number , followers and following list . You can also follow them and chat with them

Notes

  • Here you can write down your thoughts and save them . To save your note you have to give a title and date. Then you can write your thoughts

App Developers Information

  • Here you can find the information of app developers . You can contact with them via mail

Notifications

  • Here you will be notified whenever a user send you a message or follow you . You can visit their profile by clicking on them

Sign Out

  • You can sign out from the app by clicking on sign out option
